Privacy Policy

1. Privacy Commitment

We prioritize your privacy. We do not require account registration, and we do not directly collect personal information such as names, emails, or phone numbers.

2. Analytics & Advertising (Third-Party Data)

To improve game performance and provide free content, we use analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) and ad networks. These services may automatically collect:

  • Device Identifiers: IDFA, Google Advertising ID.
  • Approximate location: Country/City level.
  • Gameplay statistics: Session duration, level progress, technical logs.

3. Global Compliance

  • Saudi PDPL: We comply with the Saudi Personal Data Protection Law.
  • GDPR (EU): We process technical data based on your implied consent through game usage.
  • CCPA/CPRA (California): We do not sell your personal information. Users have the right to opt-out of sharing for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • CalOPPA: We ensure transparency regarding the technical data collected and how it is processed.

4. User Controls

You can opt-out of personalized ads or reset your advertising ID via your device's privacy settings.
